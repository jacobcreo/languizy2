Refund Policy

Effective Date: 10.10.2024

At Languizy, we strive to provide the best language learning experience. We understand that sometimes plans change, and you may need to cancel your subscription. Please read our refund policy carefully to understand your rights and obligations.

Monthly Subscriptions

Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled within the first 72 hours of the initial purchase for a full refund. After this period, the subscription will continue for the remainder of the month and will automatically renew each month until cancelled. No refunds will be issued after the initial 72-hour window.

Yearly Subscriptions

Yearly subscriptions can be cancelled within the first 14 days of the initial purchase for a full refund. After this period, the subscription will continue for the remainder of the year and will automatically renew at the same price each year until cancelled. No refunds will be issued after the initial 14-day window.

How to Cancel

To cancel your subscription, please log in to your account and navigate to the subscription settings. Follow the prompts to cancel your subscription. If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team at support@languizy.com.

Additional Information

For more details on our subscription terms, please refer to our Terms and Conditions. For pricing details, visit our Pricing page.
