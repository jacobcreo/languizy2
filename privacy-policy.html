Languizy Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10.10.2024

Welcome to Languizy, a language learning platform operated by Content Technologies. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is collected, processed, and stored in compliance with all applicable data protection laws, including the laws of Israel, the United States, the European Union, and other global regulations.

By using Languizy, you agree to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue your use of our services immediately.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

A. Information Provided by the User

When you create a user account on Languizy, you voluntarily provide the following personal identifiable information (PII):

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Profile Image

You may also voluntarily provide additional information during your engagement with the platform, including:

  • Content of Chats with AI Chat Bots, which may include further PII depending on what you choose to submit.

B. Information Automatically Collected

We collect certain data automatically, including:

  • Cookies: Our site uses cookies to enhance the user experience and perform essential functions. Cookies are required to enable certain functionalities, and by using our site, you consent to the use of cookies.
  • Google Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to track usage of our website, including user behavior, session length, and other anonymous tracking metrics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide Services: Use your PII to create and manage your account.
  • Personalize Your Learning Experience: Use the information provided in AI chats to customize your language learning experience.
  • Research and Development: The content of chats may be used for internal research to improve our platform and language learning tools.
  • Marketing Communications: We may use your email address to send promotional materials or other marketing communications. Marketing is opt-out, and each email will contain an unsubscribe link.
  • Analytics and Site Functionality: Use cookies and Google Analytics to improve site functionality and understand user interaction.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: By providing your personal data, you consent to its processing as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
  • Contractual Necessity: We require your personal data to provide the services you request.
  • Legitimate Interests: We process data for legitimate interests, such as improving our services and conducting research.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data as follows:

  • Account Data: Information such as your name, email address, and profile image is stored as long as your account is active.
  • Chat Data: Data from your interactions with the AI chat bots will be stored indefinitely unless otherwise requested by you.

5. User Rights and Data Deletion

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to Be Forgotten): You may request the deletion of your personal data by emailing us at contact@languizy.com. Deletion requests may take up to 28 business days to process, and we may require proof of your identity and ownership of the data.

Please note that some information may need to be retained for legal purposes or for purposes related to ongoing contracts.

6. Opt-Out of Marketing Communications

You may opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by:

  • Clicking the unsubscribe link in the marketing emails we send, or
  • Emailing contact@languizy.com with the subject line "Unsubscribe."

7. Sharing of Data

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your personal data under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., hosting services, analytics tools) who assist us in operating our website and services.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your data when required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Languizy uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to provide essential functions, including:

  • Session Management
  • Site Analytics (via Google Analytics)

By using our site, you agree to the use of cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, this may impact the functionality of our services.

9. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While no security measure is 100% foolproof, we strive to ensure that your data is handled securely.

10. Data Transfers

Languizy is managed by Content Technologies, an Israeli entity. By using the platform, you consent to the transfer of your personal data to Israel and potentially other jurisdictions, where data protection laws may differ from your local laws. We comply with international data protection regulations, including GDPR and CCPA.

11. Minors

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly.

13. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to exercise your rights regarding your personal data, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@languizy.com
  • Entity: Content Technologies, Tel Aviv, Israel
