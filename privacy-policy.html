Languizy Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 4, 2025

Last Updated: February 4, 2025

Welcome to Languizy, a language learning platform operated by Content Technologies (“we,” “us,” “our”). We recognize the importance of protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal data when you use our Services, which include our Website and our iOS mobile application. Please note that certain features – such as our Chat functionality – are available only on our Website and are not offered within the iOS App.

By using Languizy (collectively, the “Services”), you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this Policy, please discontinue your use of our Services immediately.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect data necessary to provide and improve our Services. The type and amount of data collected depend on how you use our Services.

A. Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account on Languizy, you voluntarily provide the following personal identifiable information (PII):

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Profile Image

You may also voluntarily provide additional information during your engagement with the platform, including the content you enter in our AI Chat feature on the Website. Note: Chat functionality is available only on our Website and is not offered in the iOS App.

B. Information Automatically Collected

We collect certain data automatically, including:

  • Cookies: Our Website uses cookies to enhance user experience and enable essential functions. By using our Website, you consent to the use of cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, this may impact functionality.
  • Analytics and Device Information (iOS App): In our App, we collect usage data such as session duration, screens visited, and device information (e.g., device model, iOS version, unique device identifiers) for the purpose of analytics and improving App functionality.
  • Third-Party Analytics: We use services such as Google Analytics (for the Website) and Firebase Analytics (for the App) to gather information about your usage in an aggregated and anonymized manner.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for various purposes, including:

  • Account Creation and Authentication: To create, manage, and secure your account.
  • Providing and Personalizing Services: To deliver tailored language courses, track progress, and personalize your learning experience.
  • AI Chat Functionality: To generate responses and improve our AI features on the Website.
  • Analytics and Performance: To analyze usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, and enhance our Website and App.
  • Marketing Communications: To send newsletters, product updates, or promotional offers via email. All marketing communications include an unsubscribe option.
  • Legal and Security: To comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, and protect our Services and users.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: Your data is processed based on your consent when you create an account or use certain features.
  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the Services you request, such as managing your course progress.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our Services and ensure security, provided that such interests are not overridden by your privacy rights.

4. Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors (e.g., hosting providers, analytics services, cloud storage, email delivery services) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality and data protection obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Business Transactions: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, reorganizations, or the sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Data may be stored and processed primarily in Israel and the United States. By using our Services, you consent to such transfers and processing in jurisdictions that may have different data protection laws.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including:

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • Chat Content: AI chat logs on the Website may be stored indefinitely or until you request deletion.
  • Analytics and Logs: Data used in aggregate or anonymized forms may be retained for longer periods for reporting and analysis.

6. User Rights and Data Deletion

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, which may include:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to Be Forgotten): Request deletion of your personal data by contacting us at contact@languizy.com. Please note that some data may be retained as required by law or contractual obligations. Deletion requests may take up to 28 business days and may require proof of identity.
  • Consent Withdrawal: You may withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by contacting us or adjusting your device/App settings where applicable.
  • Data Portability and Objection: Where applicable under local law.

7. Children and Minors

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data without appropriate consent, we will delete such data promptly.

8. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials.

9. iOS App-Specific Practices

In accordance with Apple’s App Store Review Guidelines, we follow these practices for our iOS App:

  • Privacy Policy Availability: A link to this Privacy Policy is provided in our within the App’s settings or help menu.
  • Permissions & Consent: The App will request access to device features (e.g., Photos, Camera, Microphone) only when necessary and with clear explanations. You may withdraw these permissions at any time via your device’s Settings.
  • Data Minimization: We only request data that is essential for the App’s core functionality.
  • Account Sign-In and Deletion: You may create an account using email/password or supported social logins. Account deletion can be requested from within the App’s settings or by contacting us at contact@languizy.com.
  • Third-Party SDKs: We use trusted third-party SDKs (e.g., Firebase, Google Sign-In, Facebook SDK) that are required to process your data under obligations no less protective than this Policy.
  • Data Sharing & Tracking: Personal data is not shared or tracked without your explicit consent except as required by law. Paid functionality is not contingent upon data sharing.
  • Chat Functionality: Chat features are available exclusively on our Website and are not provided in the iOS App.

10. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using our Services, you consent to such transfers. We take appropriate measures to ensure your data is protected in accordance with applicable laws (e.g., GDPR).

11. Updates to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with an updated Effective Date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@languizy.com
  • Entity: Content Technologies, Tel Aviv, Israel
